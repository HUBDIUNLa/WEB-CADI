Incubation Model: OPEN SOURCE

How to participate?

A model that enhances shared, replicable, and free solutions for collective benefit.

What is this model about?

The open source project incubator supports the development of initiatives that can be freely used, adapted, and distributed by others—individuals, communities, or organizations.

This approach promotes collaboration, transparency, and equitable access to knowledge, fostering proposals that openly share documentation, blueprints, software, or processes.

What does the support include?

  • Defining the project as an open and free solution.
  • Technical and visual documentation suitable for replication.
  • Writing licenses, user manuals, and distribution guidelines.
  • Designing strategies for access, training, and scalability.
  • Connecting with open source communities, networks, or platforms.

Who is it for?

For students, professionals, collectives, or teams developing solutions that can benefit others and are willing to share their work under open and free licenses.
