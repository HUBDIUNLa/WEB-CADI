What is Technical Assistance?

Technical assistance is a service that provides specialized support to companies and entrepreneurs in areas such as business management, product development, process improvement, and technology implementation. Its goal is to strengthen organizational capabilities and contribute to sustainable growth.

This area of the HUB aims to strengthen the local productive network through tools of design, management, and innovation. It supports economic actors in developing, improving, or rethinking their products, services, or business models.

Support may include design diagnostics, product redesign, process optimization, branding and communication strategies, sustainability, technology integration, and tailored training and technical assistance.

Our approach is based on collaboration, knowledge transfer, and co-creation. We work hand-in-hand with each organization to identify opportunities, design custom solutions, and drive growth with local impact.

We promote a comprehensive perspective that integrates design, technology, and social knowledge, understanding that sustainable development requires situated, inclusive, and innovative solutions.
