Incubation Model: CALL FOR PROJECTS

How to participate?

A tool to access public or private funding through technical or institutional calls.

What is this model about?

The call-for-projects incubator offers guidance and support for drafting, improving, and submitting projects to organizations that provide funding, technical assistance, or institutional partnerships.

It is aimed at individuals, teams, or institutions with an early-stage idea, a project under development, or a consolidated proposal suitable for submission to an open call (local, national, or international).

This model helps identify opportunities, supports strategic and technical project development, and improves submissions to increase the chances of selection and funding.

What does the support include?

  • Search and analysis of relevant calls.
  • Project evaluation and adaptation to technical requirements.
  • Assistance in completing forms and documents.
  • Writing objectives, budgets, and schedules.
  • Monitoring and support throughout the application process.

Who is this for?

This model is intended for students, graduates, faculty, entrepreneurs, or institutions seeking external resources to materialize or scale their design, innovation, technology, or social impact projects.
