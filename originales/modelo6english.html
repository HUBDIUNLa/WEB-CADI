Incubation Model: ENTREPRENEURSHIP / ENTERPRISE

How to participate?

Turn your idea or project into a technological, creative or productive enterprise with real impact.

What is this model about?

This model supports students, graduates or teams looking to transform their academic, productive or creative project into a real enterprise with growth potential.

It provides tools for management, business model validation, market strategies and enterprise development.

Support includes:

  • Business model definition (Canvas, Lean, etc.).
  • Market research and user profiling.
  • Brand identity, communication and digital presence.
  • Cost planning, investment and scalability strategies.
  • Support with legal procedures, licenses or registrations.

Who is it for?

For those who already have an advanced product, service or idea and want to consolidate, scale and professionalize their activity within the real productive system.
