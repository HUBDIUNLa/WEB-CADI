Incubation Model: PRODUCT LAUNCH

How to participate?

Launch your product, service or line with strategic and professional support.

What is this model about?

The launch incubator is designed for those who already have a developed product, service or series and want to effectively bring it to market. It supports the planning, execution and evaluation of a launch strategy.

It's aimed at students, graduates or entrepreneurs who want to validate their proposal with real users, gain visibility and positioning, and begin generating sales or adoption.

What does the support include?

  • Definition of target segments and audience.
  • Development of branding, communication and visual pieces.
  • Launch plan design: objectives, channels and timeline.
  • Digital and face-to-face dissemination strategies.
  • Impact assessment and post-launch follow-up.

Who is it for?

For those with a consolidated proposal who need to formally present and promote it to their market or community, aiming to generate interest, contacts, sales or institutional impact.
