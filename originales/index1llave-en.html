What is a Turnkey Project?

A turnkey project is a comprehensive project management approach where the provider assumes full responsibility for the design, development, and delivery of a fully functional product or service. In this model, the client receives a finished, ready-to-use solution without needing to be involved in technical or logistical details.

A turnkey project is an all-in-one solution designed for immediate and hassle-free implementation. It’s a complete package from design to execution, aimed at efficiently solving a specific need with coherence and guaranteed results.

At the HUB, turnkey projects are custom developments tailored to the characteristics, objectives, and context of each client. Each project is conceived with scalability in mind, allowing for replication or franchising in other settings while maintaining its core essence and functionality.

These solutions include product or service design, communication strategies, visual identity, technical planning, investment estimation, and operational schemes. Everything in a single package ready to launch.

Turnkey projects are ideal for entrepreneurs, cooperatives, SMEs, and institutions seeking to implement high-impact value propositions in their communities by leveraging the HUB team’s design, technological, and strategic knowledge.
