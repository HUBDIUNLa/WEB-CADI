Incubation Model: COMPETITION

How to participate?

A way to represent UNLa through unique and competitive projects in national or international calls.

What does this model involve?

This competition incubator provides comprehensive guidance and support for submitting projects to design, innovation, development, or entrepreneurship competitions.

It is aimed at students, graduates, entrepreneurs, or work teams with an outstanding proposal, whether under development or fully formulated, with potential for national or international competitions.

Through this model, the representation of UNLa or sponsoring entities is encouraged, enhancing the public projection of innovative, sustainable, and impactful projects.

What does the support include?

  • Competition analysis and requirements.
  • Project review and improvement suggestions.
  • Advice on technical and visual presentation.
  • Preparation of documentation, texts, and pitch.
  • Registration management and institutional linkage.

Who is it for?

This model is ideal for individuals or teams with a developing project who want visibility, validation, and competitive reach. It is also an opportunity to represent UNLa in high-level academic and professional contexts.
